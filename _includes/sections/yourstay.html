Transportation & Parking

To get to the event grounds, we've left details below on a parking lot that we've reserved down the street as well as taxi contact details. To get home at the end of the night, we'll have a shuttle van available and as many taxi's as we can wrangle to shuttle people back to their beds. If you drove to the event, feel free to leave your car overnight, and return for it the next day :)

Parking

We've reserved parking spaces at the Bayside Oral Surgery Centre, just up the road on the corner of Bridge & Chatham. These spaces need to be vacated by Sunday at 2:00pm, so please be sure to return for your car before then. If spaces fill up, there is lots of street parking on adjacent streets, and a number of businesses on Dundas that are a short walk away. Please note parking on the east side of Bleecker, and on Bridge street is not permitted.

Taxis

For anyone who has experienced Belleville Taxis before, they can be a bit... inconsistent. We recommend reserving your cab and arriving a bit early, just in case.
